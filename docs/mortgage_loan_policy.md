# Mortgage Loan Policy

Mortgage loans are secured loans used to purchase or refinance residential real estate.

## Property Types
- Primary residence
- Second homes
- Investment properties (restricted)

## Loan Terms
- 15-year fixed
- 30-year fixed
- Adjustable-rate mortgages (ARM)

## Down Payment
- Minimum 3% for qualified borrowers
- Minimum 20% to avoid PMI

Mortgage approval is subject to appraisal and underwriting review.
