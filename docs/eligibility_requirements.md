# Eligibility Requirements

All loan applicants must meet the following criteria:

## Basic Eligibility
- Minimum age: 18
- U.S. citizen or permanent resident
- Valid Social Security Number

## Credit Requirements
- Minimum credit score: 620 for personal loans
- Minimum credit score: 680 for mortgage loans

## Income Requirements
- Verifiable income required
- Self-employed applicants must provide 2 years of tax returns

Meeting eligibility requirements does not guarantee approval.
