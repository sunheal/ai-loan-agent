# Credit Score Exceptions

In certain cases, applicants with credit scores below standard thresholds may still be considered.

## Exception Criteria
Exceptions may be granted if:
- The applicant has a strong income history
- The applicant has low overall debt
- A qualified co-signer is added

## Limitations
- Exceptions are not guaranteed
- Final approval is at underwriting discretion
- Mortgage loans have stricter exception criteria

Credit score exceptions do not apply to applicants with recent bankruptcies.
