# Interest Rates

Interest rates vary based on loan type, credit profile, and market conditions.

## Personal Loans
- Fixed APR range: 6.99% – 24.99%

## Auto Loans
- New vehicles: 5.49% – 12.99%
- Used vehicles: 6.99% – 15.99%

## Mortgage Loans
- Rates determined daily
- Subject to market fluctuations

Rates are not guaranteed until loan approval.
