# Personal Loan Policy

Personal loans are unsecured installment loans designed for general consumer use.

## Loan Amounts
- Minimum: $1,000
- Maximum: $50,000

## Loan Terms
- 24 to 60 months

## Usage Restrictions
Personal loans may not be used for:
- Business purposes
- Real estate purchases
- Cryptocurrency investments

## Approval Criteria
Approval is based on:
- Credit score
- Income verification
- Debt-to-income (DTI) ratio

Personal loans do not require collateral.
